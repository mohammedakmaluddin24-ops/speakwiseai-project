# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to providing a welcoming and respectful environment for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

- Be considerate and polite.
- Respect differing viewpoints.
- Refrain from abusive or discriminatory language.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, hate speech, or personal attacks.
- Discrimination on the basis of race, gender, religion, or any protected characteristic.
- Disruptive or hostile behavior in discussions.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please contact the project maintainers.
